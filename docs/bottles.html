Product Details

The Beginner’s Equipment Kit comes complete with supplies for sanitation, brewing, and fermenting your beer. The kit is a one-time purchase that will give you the tools to brew many batches of beer at home. We’ve included the following: - 1-gallon glass carboy (a perfect fermenter for beginners) - A specially designed cap and airlock (no need to fuss over a slippery rubber stopper) - Auto-siphon + tube - Bottle filler - Digital thermometer - Mesh Brew-in-a-Bag (BIAB) - StellarSan (a homebrewer’s best friend - non-rinse sanitizer) - Sodium percarbonate (perfect for easy cleaning!)

Every time you want to brew a new batch o beer, all you’ll need to do is refill your ingredients. Since beer style is largely dependent on the ingredients and technique, this kit can be used to brew most, if not all, styles of beer!
